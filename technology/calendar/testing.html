Recapping TDD and Testing Along the Spectrum

You know you should TDD, but do you struggle where to start? What's the difference between a unit, integration and end-to-end testing? And how do you write tests for existing code? Throughout this workshop we'll explore these ideas and more. Hopefully you'll come away with a greater appreciation for TDD, and more ways to embed it into your coding practice.

This is suitable for any level of Engineer at Made Tech, and will involve lots of discussion, pairing and coding.

Please bear in mind that the workshop will focus strongly on the in-person experience.

London, Tuesday PM 24th October (online) and Wednesday 25th October (in person)

A 1.5 day bootcamp aimed to build confidence in TDD, use different tools across different areas of testing, and how to bug fix through testing. Suitable for all levels. Please note: Part 1 will be online, Parts 2 and 3 will be in person. You need to be able to attend all three parts.
